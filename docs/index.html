What it does

🐤

Wanders your screen

The chick idles, walks, and bounces around all your displays — including across multi-monitor setups.

🏠

Chicken coop island

Drop a draggable coop onto any space. Click it to spawn coloured chick friends. They wander home periodically.

🎵

Synthesised voices

22 chirp variants generated live from real-world bioacoustic data — not pre-recorded samples. Each click sounds slightly different.

🌈

Eight colour variations

Click the coop to summon chicks in pink, mint, lavender, blue, red, white, brown, and the original yellow.

🪶

Click to chirp

Click on a critter to make it peep. Move the cursor near it and watch it dart away in a panic.

⚡

Practically free

Idle chicks consume no CPU. Active animation is around 1–3 % on a single core. Stays out of your way.

First-time install — read this if macOS blocks the app
  1. Unzip Chick.zip and drag Chick.app into ~/Applications.
  2. The app is unsigned, so on first launch macOS will refuse it. Right-click → Open, then click Open again in the dialog.
  3. The chick appears in the centre of your screen. A 🐤 icon shows up in the menu bar with all controls (spawn, despawn, change style, show coop, quit).

Or build from source: git clone && ./build.sh && open Chick.app

Built with Swift & AppKit · Sounds synthesised from bioacoustic research · Sprite styles generated with AI image tools and processed in Pillow
